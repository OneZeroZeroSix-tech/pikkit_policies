Pikkit – Risk Disclosure Statement

1. PURPOSE AND FUNCTION OF THIS DISCLOSURE

This Risk Disclosure Statement (“Disclosure”) is provided to inform users, senders, recipients, couriers, and other participants (collectively, “Users”) of the inherent, foreseeable, and unforeseeable risks associated with the use of a delivery facilitation platform.

The purpose of this Disclosure is to:

  • Explicitly warn Users of known and unknown risks

  • Ensure informed consent prior to platform use

  • Allocate responsibility and assumption of risk

  • Reduce claims of negligence, failure to warn, and misrepresentation

  • Support contractual risk allocation and limitation of liability

  • Comply with common-law and statutory duty-to-warn standards

Use of the Platform constitutes acknowledgment and acceptance of these risks.

2. NATURE OF THE PLATFORM

The Platform is a technology service that facilitates connections between independent couriers and users requesting delivery services.

The Platform:

  • Does not transport goods itself

  • Does not employ couriers as employees

  • Does not supervise deliveries in real time

  • Does not guarantee outcomes, timing, or safety

All deliveries involve third-party individuals operating independently.

3. GENERAL DELIVERY RISKS

Users acknowledge that delivery services inherently involve risks, including but not limited to:

  • Loss, theft, or damage of items

  • Delays, rerouting, or non-completion

  • Misdelivery or delivery to unintended recipients

  • Weather-related disruptions

  • Mechanical failures

  • Human error

Such risks exist regardless of precautions taken.

4. PHYSICAL SAFETY RISKS

4.1 Courier Safety Risks

Couriers may be exposed to:

  • Traffic accidents

  • Road hazards

  • Poor weather conditions

  • Unsafe neighborhoods or locations

  • Assault, robbery, or harassment

  • Fatigue or physical injury

The Platform does not control road conditions, third-party behavior, or environmental hazards.

4.2 Sender and Recipient Risks

Senders and recipients may encounter:

  • Interactions with unknown individuals

  • Property damage during pickup or delivery

  • Personal safety risks during handoffs

  • Trespass or access disputes

Users are responsible for ensuring safe pickup and delivery environments.

5. VEHICLE-RELATED RISKS

Deliveries may involve personal vehicles, bicycles, scooters, or walking couriers.

Risks include:

  • Vehicle accidents

  • Improper vehicle maintenance

  • Inadequate insurance coverage

  • Mechanical failures

  • Traffic law violations

The Platform does not inspect or certify vehicles.

6. ITEM-RELATED RISKS

6.1 Fragile and Valuable Items

Items may be damaged due to:

  • Handling errors

  • Vibration or movement

  • Temperature exposure

  • Improper packaging

Users are solely responsible for proper packaging.

6.2 Perishable Items

Food, pharmaceuticals, or temperature-sensitive items may:

  • Spoil

  • Become unsafe for consumption

  • Lose efficacy

The Platform does not guarantee temperature control.

6.3 Prohibited or Restricted Items

Attempting to ship prohibited items increases legal and safety risks.

The Platform disclaims liability arising from prohibited shipments.

7. LEGAL AND REGULATORY RISKS

Users acknowledge risks related to:

  • Violation of local, state, or federal laws

  • Improper shipment of regulated goods

  • Customs or border issues

  • Seizure by authorities

  • Fines, penalties, or criminal charges

Users are solely responsible for legal compliance.

8. CRIMINAL AND THIRD-PARTY RISKS

Despite screening efforts, risks include:

  • Fraudulent users

  • Criminal activity

  • Impersonation

  • Theft or deception

  • Misuse of the Platform

The Platform cannot eliminate all criminal risks.

9. TECHNOLOGY AND SYSTEM RISKS

The Platform relies on software and networks subject to:

  • Outages

  • Bugs or errors

  • Data transmission failures

  • GPS inaccuracies

  • Cybersecurity threats

Service interruptions may occur without notice.

10. COMMUNICATION RISKS

In-app messaging and communications may:

  • Be delayed

  • Be misunderstood

  • Contain inaccurate information

  • Be unavailable due to technical issues

Users should not rely exclusively on Platform communications for critical decisions.

11. INSURANCE AND FINANCIAL RISKS

The Platform does not provide comprehensive insurance coverage for:

  • Personal injury

Any insurance coverage is limited and subject to exclusions.

Users are encouraged to obtain independent insurance.

The Platform makes no guarantees regarding:

  • Delivery completion

  • Timing

  • Condition of items

  • Courier conduct

  • Availability of couriers

All services are provided on an “as-is” and “as-available” basis.

13. ASSUMPTION OF RISK

By using the Platform, Users knowingly and voluntarily assume all risks associated with:

  • Requesting deliveries

  • Acting as couriers

  • Interacting with third parties

This assumption applies to both known and unknown risks.

The Platform’s role is limited to facilitating connections.

The Platform does not:

  • Assume custody of items

Users bear primary responsibility for their actions and decisions.
