1. Introduction and Purpose

Pykkit (“Company,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information of all users (“Users,” “you,” or “your”) who access or use the Pykkit website, mobile applications, and related services (collectively, the “Platform”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your personal information in connection with your use of the Platform.

This Privacy Policy forms an integral part of the Pykkit Terms of Service & User Agreement. By accessing or using the Platform, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any portion of this Policy, you must discontinue use of the Platform.

The purpose of this Privacy Policy is to promote transparency, ensure compliance with applicable privacy laws, and explain your rights and choices regarding your personal data.

2. Scope of This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all Users of the Platform, including but not limited to:

  • Senders requesting deliveries

  • Couriers performing deliveries

  • Receivers of deliveries

  • Visitors browsing the Platform without creating an account

This Policy applies only to information collected through the Platform and does not apply to third-party websites, services, or applications that may be linked to or integrated with the Platform.

3. Definitions

For purposes of this Privacy Policy:

  • Personal Information means any information that identifies, relates to, describes, or can reasonably be linked to an identifiable individual.

  • Sensitive Personal Information includes government identification numbers, financial account details, precise geolocation data, and identity verification information.

  • Processing means any operation performed on personal information, including collection, storage, use, disclosure, or deletion.

Capitalized terms not defined here have the meanings assigned in the Terms of Service.

4. Information We Collect

4.1 Information You Provide Directly

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you interact with the Platform, including but not limited to:

  • Full name

  • Email address

  • Phone number

  • Mailing address

  • Pickup and delivery addresses

  • Account login credentials

  • Payment-related information (processed via third-party providers)

  • Communications with customer support

  • Uploaded documents for verification or insurance claims

This information is necessary to create accounts, facilitate deliveries, process payments, resolve disputes, and comply with legal obligations.

4.2 Courier-Specific Information

If you register as a Courier, we may collect additional information, including:

  • Driver’s license details

  • Vehicle information

  • Proof of vehicle insurance

  • Background check results

  • Identity verification documents

  • Tax reporting information

This information is collected to ensure safety, legal compliance, and eligibility to perform delivery services.

4.3 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the Platform, we automatically collect certain technical and usage data, such as:

  • IP address

  • Device type and operating system

  • Browser type

  • App usage data

  • Access times and pages viewed

  • Approximate or precise location data (when enabled)

This information helps us improve functionality, detect fraud, ensure security, and optimize user experience.

4.4 Location Information

Location data may be collected to:

  • Enable real-time delivery tracking

  • Match Senders with nearby Couriers

  • Improve route efficiency

  • Prevent fraud or misuse

You may disable location services through your device settings, though doing so may limit Platform functionality.

5. How We Use Your Information

We use personal information for the following purposes:

  • To create, manage, and authenticate user accounts

  • To facilitate delivery requests and courier matching

  • To process payments and payouts

  • To provide tracking and status updates

  • To communicate with Users regarding services, updates, or issues

  • To investigate disputes, claims, or suspected misconduct

  • To comply with legal, regulatory, and tax obligations

  • To enforce our Terms of Service and other policies

We only process personal information where we have a lawful basis to do so.

6. Sharing of Personal Information

We do not sell personal information. However, we may share information in the following circumstances:

6.1 With Other Users

  • Pickup and delivery details are shared with Couriers

  • Limited contact information may be shared to complete deliveries

6.2 With Service Providers

We may share information with trusted third parties who assist us with:

  • Payment processing

  • Identity verification

  • Background checks

  • Cloud hosting

  • Analytics and security services

These providers are contractually required to protect your data and use it only for authorized purposes.

6.3 Legal and Compliance Disclosures

We may disclose personal information if required to:

  • Comply with legal obligations or court orders

  • Respond to lawful requests from law enforcement

  • Protect the rights, safety, or property of Users or the Company

  • Investigate fraud or illegal activity

  • Communication records

This information is used solely to evaluate claims and resolve disputes in accordance with the Insurance Policy.
