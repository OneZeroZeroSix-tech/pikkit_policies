Pikkit – Courier Independent Contractor Agreement

Effective Date: January 10, 2026

Last Updated: January 10, 2026

This Courier Independent Contractor Agreement (“Agreement”) is entered into by and between Pikkit, a technology platform company (“Company,” “Pikkit,” “Platform”), and the individual or entity registering as a courier on the Platform (“Courier”). This Agreement governs Courier’s access to and use of the Platform for the purpose of accepting and performing delivery requests.

By registering for, accessing, or using the Platform as a Courier, Courier acknowledges that Courier has read, understood, and agrees to be bound by this Agreement in its entirety. If Courier does not agree to these terms, Courier must not access or use the Platform.

1. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR RELATIONSHIP

Courier expressly acknowledges and agrees that Courier is an independent contractor and not an employee, agent, joint venturer, partner, or representative of the Company for any purpose. Nothing in this Agreement shall be construed to create any employment relationship, agency relationship, or fiduciary relationship between Courier and the Company.

Courier shall have no authority to bind the Company, incur obligations on behalf of the Company, or represent to any third party that Courier is an employee or agent of the Company. Courier shall not wear uniforms, display signage, or otherwise present themselves as an employee of the Company except as expressly permitted for identification purposes through the Platform.

Courier acknowledges that this Agreement is intended to comply with applicable California law, including but not limited to Proposition 22, and that Courier retains control over the manner and means of performing delivery services.

2. NO EMPLOYEE BENEFITS

Courier acknowledges and agrees that Courier is not entitled to any employee benefits from the Company, including but not limited to wages, overtime pay, minimum wage, paid time off, sick leave, workers’ compensation, unemployment insurance, health insurance, retirement benefits, or any other benefit typically associated with employment.

Courier expressly waives any claim to such benefits and agrees that Courier is solely responsible for securing any insurance, benefits, or protections desired.

3. TAX RESPONSIBILITY

Courier acknowledges and agrees that Courier is solely responsible for all federal, state, and local taxes arising from payments received through the Platform, including but not limited to income taxes, self-employment taxes, payroll taxes, and any required reporting obligations.

The Company shall not withhold taxes on Courier’s behalf and shall issue any required tax reporting forms as required by law. Courier agrees to indemnify the Company against any claims, penalties, or liabilities arising from Courier’s failure to comply with tax obligations.

4. RIGHT TO ACCEPT OR DECLINE DELIVERY REQUESTS

Courier retains the absolute right to accept or decline any delivery request made available through the Platform. Courier is under no obligation to accept any minimum number of delivery requests and may cease using the Platform at any time.

Courier retains discretion over when, where, and for how long Courier chooses to make themselves available on the Platform, subject only to Platform availability and compliance with applicable rules.

5. CONTROL OVER PERFORMANCE OF SERVICES

Courier retains sole control over the manner and means of performing delivery services, including route selection, scheduling, and delivery methods, subject to compliance with applicable laws, safety requirements, and Platform policies.

The Company does not supervise, direct, or control the physical performance of Courier’s delivery services and does not dictate how Courier completes deliveries, except as required to ensure Platform integrity and legal compliance.

6. USE OF PERSONAL VEHICLE AND EQUIPMENT

Courier shall provide, at Courier’s own expense, all vehicles, equipment, tools, mobile devices, fuel, maintenance, and other resources necessary to perform delivery services.

Courier acknowledges that the Company does not provide vehicles, insurance, fuel, or equipment and assumes no responsibility for vehicle condition, maintenance, or operation.

Courier is solely responsible for ensuring that any vehicle used is legally registered, properly maintained, and compliant with all applicable laws.

7. INSURANCE REQUIREMENTS

Courier acknowledges and agrees that Courier is solely responsible for maintaining any insurance required by law, including but not limited to automobile liability insurance.

Courier acknowledges that the Company does not provide automobile insurance, workers’ compensation insurance, or cargo insurance to Couriers. Courier understands that Courier is not insured under any Platform insurance policy unless expressly stated otherwise in writing.

Courier agrees to provide proof of insurance upon request and acknowledges that failure to maintain required insurance may result in suspension or termination of Platform access.

8. COMPLIANCE WITH LAWS

Courier agrees to comply with all applicable federal, state, and local laws, regulations, and ordinances, including traffic laws, safety regulations, licensing requirements, and transportation laws.

Courier shall be solely responsible for any violations, citations, fines, penalties, or legal consequences arising from Courier’s conduct while performing delivery services.

9. PROHIBITED CONDUCT AND ITEMS

Courier agrees not to accept, transport, or deliver any items prohibited by law or Platform policy, including illegal, unsafe, or restricted goods.

Courier agrees to refuse delivery requests involving prohibited items and to report such requests to the Company.

Courier acknowledges that knowingly transporting prohibited items may result in immediate termination and legal consequences.

10. BACKGROUND CHECKS AND ELIGIBILITY

Courier acknowledges that the Company may conduct background checks, identity verification, and driving record reviews as permitted by law.

Courier consents to such checks and understands that failure to meet eligibility criteria may result in denial, suspension, or termination of Platform access.

11. PAYMENT AND FEES

Courier shall be compensated for completed deliveries in accordance with Platform policies in effect at the time of delivery.

Courier acknowledges that payment amounts, timing, and fees are determined by the Platform and may change from time to time.

Courier agrees that no payment is owed for incomplete, canceled, or rejected deliveries except as expressly stated by Platform policy.

12. INDEMNIFICATION

Courier agrees to defend, indemnify, and hold harmless the Company, its officers, directors, employees, agents, and affiliates from and against any and all claims, damages, losses, liabilities, costs, and expenses arising out of or related to Courier’s conduct, including but not limited to:

  • Courier’s performance of delivery services;

  • Courier’s violation of laws or regulations;

  • Courier’s failure to maintain insurance;

  • Courier’s misrepresentation of status;

  • Courier’s negligent, reckless, or intentional acts.

This obligation survives termination of this Agreement.

13. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

To the maximum extent permitted by law, the Company shall not be liable for any indirect, incidental, consequential, special, or punitive damages arising out of or related to this Agreement or Courier’s use of the Platform.

The Company’s total liability, if any, shall not exceed amounts actually paid to Courier through the Platform during the three (3) months preceding the event giving rise to the claim.

14. TERM AND TERMINATION

This Agreement shall remain in effect until terminated by either party.

The Company may suspend or terminate Courier’s access to the Platform at any time for violation of this Agreement, Platform policies, safety concerns, or legal compliance.

Termination does not relieve Courier of obligations accrued prior to termination.

15. DISPUTE RESOLUTION AND ARBITRATION

Courier agrees that any dispute arising out of or related to this Agreement shall be resolved through binding arbitration in accordance with the Platform’s arbitration policy, except where prohibited by law.

Courier waives the right to participate in class actions or representative proceedings to the extent permitted by law.

16. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of California, without regard to conflict-of-laws principles.

17. SEVERABILITY

If any provision of this Agreement is held invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.

18. ENTIRE AGREEMENT

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties regarding Courier’s relationship with the Company and supersedes all prior agreements or understandings.

No oral statements or representations shall modify this Agreement.

19. ACKNOWLEDGMENT

By accessing or using the Platform as a Courier, Courier acknowledges that Courier has read, understood, and voluntarily agreed to this Independent Contractor Agreement.
