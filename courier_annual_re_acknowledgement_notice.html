Pikkit – Courier Annual Re-Acknowledgement Notice

Effective Date: January 10, 2026

Last Updated: January 10, 2026

Purpose: To ensure ongoing informed consent and acknowledgment of all Platform policies, procedures, and legal agreements.

1. ANNUAL CONFIRMATION REQUIREMENT

All Couriers must annually reaffirm:

  • Understanding of the Independent Contractor status

  • Receipt and comprehension of Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, and other governing documents

  • Awareness of Proposition 22 earnings and benefit policies (California)

  • Acceptance of risk, insurance obligations, and indemnification responsibilities

2. REVIEW AND AFFIRMATION

Courier is required to:

  • Review all incorporated agreements and policies on the Platform

  • Electronically acknowledge continued acceptance

  • Certify that personal information is accurate and current

This process ensures that Couriers maintain awareness of legal and operational obligations.

3. SAFETY AND INCIDENT REVIEW

As part of the re-acknowledgment:

  • Couriers confirm understanding of safety policies

  • Couriers report any unresolved incidents or claims

  • Couriers reaffirm that Platform safety guidance has been reviewed

4. INSURANCE AND LIABILITY RECONFIRMATION

Courier annually confirms that:

  • Personal insurance coverage is maintained

  • Optional Platform-provided insurance, if applicable, has been reviewed

  • Any changes to vehicle, equipment, or insurance status have been reported

5. CONSEQUENCES OF NON-COMPLIANCE

Failure to complete the Annual Re-Acknowledgment may result in:

  • Suspension or termination of Platform access

  • Ineligibility for earnings or bonuses until completion

  • Continued liability for obligations and indemnifications under previous agreements

6. AFFIRMATION

By completing the Annual Re-Acknowledgment, Courier affirms:

  • Understanding of risks, obligations, and policies

  • Agreement to continue participation under the terms of all governing agreements

  • Commitment to maintain compliance with safety, insurance, and legal requirements

Courier’s electronic signature or acknowledgment carries the same legal effect as a handwritten signature.
