Pikkit – Courier Safety Incident Policy

1. PURPOSE

This Safety Incident Policy establishes mandatory procedures for identifying, reporting, investigating, and responding to safety incidents involving Couriers. Its objectives include:

  • Protecting users, couriers, and the public

  • Reducing negligence and liability exposure

  • Supporting insurance claims and underwriting

  • Demonstrating reasonable care and risk mitigation

2. SCOPE

This Policy applies to all Couriers during:

  • Active deliveries

  • Travel to or from pickup or drop-off

  • Any activity reasonably connected to Platform services

3. DEFINITION OF SAFETY INCIDENT

A Safety Incident includes, but is not limited to:

  • Vehicle accidents

  • Personal injury

  • Property damage

  • Theft or loss

  • Physical altercations

  • Harassment or threats

  • Law enforcement involvement

  • Hazardous material exposure

4. IMMEDIATE COURIER OBLIGATIONS

Upon any Safety Incident, the Courier must:

  • Prioritize personal and public safety

  • Contact emergency services if necessary

  • Secure the scene

  • Notify the Platform promptly

  • Preserve evidence

  • Cooperate with investigations

Failure to report is grounds for termination.

5. PLATFORM RESPONSE

Upon notification, the Platform may:

  • Temporarily suspend the Courier

  • Collect statements and documentation

  • Notify insurers

  • Coordinate with law enforcement

  • Assess ongoing risk

Suspension during investigation does not imply fault.

6. INVESTIGATION PROCESS

Investigations may include:

  • Review of GPS and app data

  • Witness statements

  • Photos and videos

  • Police reports

  • Insurance documentation

The Platform reserves sole discretion in findings.

7. OUTCOMES AND REMEDIAL ACTION

Outcomes may include:

  • Reactivation

  • Mandatory retraining

  • Permanent deactivation

  • Reporting to insurers

  • Reporting to authorities

8. COURIER COOPERATION REQUIREMENT

Couriers must:

  • Provide truthful statements

  • Respond within required timelines

  • Submit documentation

  • Attend interviews if requested

Non-cooperation may result in permanent deactivation.

9. INSURANCE AND CLAIMS

Couriers acknowledge:

  • Personal insurance is primary

  • Platform insurance, if any, is secondary or limited

  • Failure to maintain insurance may void coverage

  • Claims must be timely and accurate

10. CONFIDENTIALITY

Incident information is confidential and may be disclosed only to:

  • Insurers

  • Law enforcement

  • Legal counsel

  • Regulators as required

11. NON-RETALIATION

Couriers will not be retaliated against for good-faith reporting of safety incidents.

False or malicious reports may result in disciplinary action.

12. POLICY ENFORCEMENT

This Policy is enforced strictly to:

  • Demonstrate reasonable care

  • Reduce foreseeable harm

  • Protect the Platform legally and operationally

13. POLICY LIMITATIONS

This Policy does not guarantee:

  • Prevention of all harm

  • Coverage for all losses

  • Continued access to the Platform

14. UPDATES AND MODIFICATIONS

The Platform may update this Policy at any time. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
