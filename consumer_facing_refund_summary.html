Pikkit – Consumer-Facing Refund Summary

Purpose:

To provide a clear, plain-language explanation for Users while remaining fully consistent with the authoritative Refund Policy.

This summary does not replace the full Policy.

1. WHEN REFUNDS ARE POSSIBLE

Refunds may be issued only if:

  • A delivery was canceled early

  • A verified billing error occurred

  • A delivery was not completed through no fault of the Sender

  • Fraud is confirmed

Refunds are not guaranteed.

2. WHEN REFUNDS ARE NOT AVAILABLE

Refunds are generally not issued for:

  • Completed deliveries

  • Late deliveries due to traffic or weather

  • Change of mind

  • Dissatisfaction without proof

  • Violations of Platform rules

3. CANCELLATION TIMING MATTERS

  • Before Courier accepts → possible full refund

  • After acceptance → partial refund possible

  • After pickup → refunds are rare

4. EVIDENCE IS REQUIRED

We may ask for:

  • Photos

  • Messages

  • Receipts

  • Delivery details

Without evidence, claims may be denied.

5. CHARGEBACKS

Please contact us before filing a chargeback.

Unapproved chargebacks may result in account suspension.

6. FINAL DECISIONS

All refund decisions are final unless required otherwise by law.
