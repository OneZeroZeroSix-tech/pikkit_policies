Pykkit – Courier Arbitration Opt-Out Form

Effective Date: January 10, 2026

Last Updated: January 10, 2026

Purpose: To provide Couriers an opportunity to opt out of mandatory arbitration provisions under the governing agreements, in compliance with applicable law.

1. INTRODUCTION

Courier may be required under the Courier Independent Contractor Agreement and other Platform agreements to resolve disputes through arbitration rather than litigation. Arbitration is binding and may limit certain rights.

Courier may opt out of this provision by completing and submitting this form within the time frame specified by the Platform.

2. ARBITRATION DESCRIPTION

Courier acknowledges that arbitration:

  • Replaces jury trials and court proceedings for most disputes

  • Is conducted under rules established by the governing arbitration body

  • May limit the ability to join class actions or collective claims

  • Is final and binding except in limited circumstances

3. OPT-OUT INSTRUCTIONS

To opt out:

  • Complete all fields on this form accurately

  • Submit via the designated Platform submission method (email, portal, or certified mail)

  • Submission must occur within 30 days of account creation or acceptance of the governing agreements

Failure to timely submit the opt-out form will result in the mandatory arbitration provision remaining in effect.

4. CONFIRMATION AND ACKNOWLEDGMENT

Courier certifies that:

  • They have read and understood the arbitration provisions

  • They voluntarily choose to opt out

  • They understand that opting out does not affect any other contractual obligations

Courier signature (electronic or handwritten) confirms acknowledgment and election.
